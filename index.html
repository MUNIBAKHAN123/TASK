Description

"Neuschwastein Castle". Extraordinary piece of metalwork that reproduces the famous Neuschwanstein Castle, ordered to be built by Ludwig II of Bavaria. Handcrafted by 5 goldsmiths and jewelers for 3 years. Composed of 741 pieces joined by 1557 inner nuts, 360 windows decorated with enamel. Duralado antioxidant treatment (fire varnished). It sits on a base surrounded by quartz crystallizations. Weight 52 Kg. Piece of extraordinary size. 65x40x115cm
